Boat Exam and Study

Here are the most commonly missed questions!

  1. Air moves from ______________ to ______________? A:high, low
  2. The Q flag used for foreign port entry means: A: Vessel is Healthy
  3. True or false: One desirable feature of LPG is that it liquefies at a relatively low pressure, allowing low-cost tanks to contain a large amount of energy because liquid is much denser than gas. A: True
  4. True or false: AIS uses VHF radio frequencies to broadcast the AIS information. A: True
  5. When power-driven vessels are in sight of one another and meeting or crossing at a distance within half a mile of each other, each vessel must use sound signals. What do two short horn blasts from another vessel mean in a meeting situation? A:Two short horn blasts from another vessel mean they intend to pass starboard side to starboard side.
  6. True or false: It makes little difference whether statute miles or nautical miles are selected during setup of the GPS, because they are very similar. A:False
  7. What does a Cunningham do? A:Changes location of draft in the main sail.
